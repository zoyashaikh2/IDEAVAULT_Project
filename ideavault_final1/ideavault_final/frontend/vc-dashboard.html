How the VC Intelligence System Works

IdeaVault simulates real-world venture capital flows without actual money exchanging hands. Here is a guide to the tools at your disposal:

  • Virtual Cheque & Pipeline: When you track a deal or founders launch a "Funding Round", the amounts (e.g., ₹50,00,000) are purely simulation parameters in Indian Rupees (₹). This allows founders to gauge interest and rehearse ticket sizes, and VCs to track their mock portfolios before real negotiations begin.
  • Rigorous Multidimensional Logistic Classifier: This is a server-side AI/ML model that processes real engagement metrics. It calculates "Exit Score" and "Success %" using a Sigmoid function bounded from 0–99. It dynamically weighs the idea's platform views, user likes, AI-evaluated innovation score, and VC-thesis fit to mathematically estimate probability of an eventual exit.
  • ROI Simulation (No Tokens): The ROI simulator calculates returns instantly on your browser using a deterministic math model rather than calling a language model. It takes your hypothetical Valuation, Cheque size, and Equity ask, combines it with the startup's Traction score, and projects the startup's 5-year valuation multiple and your stake's future value.
