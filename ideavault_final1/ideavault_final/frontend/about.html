About IdeaVault

Where Ideas Take Shape — An AI-powered startup intelligence platform that turns raw concepts into validated, fundable ventures.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe every great startup begins with a single idea. IdeaVault exists to democratize startup intelligence — giving every founder, student, and innovator access to the same caliber of market analysis, competitive intelligence, and strategic roadmapping that was once reserved for well-funded ventures. Our AI doesn't just generate suggestions — it performs real market analysis, sentiment evaluation, risk assessment, and investor matching using advanced natural language processing and machine learning techniques.

✨ What We Offer

AI Strategic Analysis

Real-time market sizing, SWOT analysis, competitive landscape mapping, and innovation scoring powered by Claude AI — no static templates.

AI Idea Generator

Describe what you want to build, and our AI identifies market gaps, innovation angles, and crafts a unique startup concept with target customers.

Dynamic Roadmaps

AI generates 4-phase launch roadmaps with budgets, timelines, team requirements, and KPIs — tailored to your specific idea and market.

VC Matching Engine

AI scores your idea against real investor profiles, ranking fit by sector, stage, and ticket size. Get actionable pitch angles for each VC.

Duplicate Detection

Jaccard similarity + AI verification ensures your idea is original. Get differentiation strategies if overlap is detected.

Sentiment Analytics

NLP-powered community feedback analysis with positive/neutral/negative breakdowns, key themes, and AI recommendations.

🧠 AI/ML Techniques Used

Natural Language Processing

Claude AI processes and understands idea descriptions to generate contextual market analysis, SWOT breakdowns, and strategic summaries.

Sentiment Analysis

Comment-level sentiment classification with polarity scoring (-100 to 100), theme extraction, and actionable founder recommendations.

Jaccard Similarity + AI Verification

Text similarity using set intersection metrics, combined with LLM-based nuanced overlap verification for authenticity checking.

Composite Scoring Algorithm

Multi-factor ranking combining likes, comments, views, ratings, and AI innovation scores with weighted coefficients for idea ranking.

VC Fit Scoring

Per-investor AI scoring (0-100) analyzing sector alignment, stage fit, ticket compatibility, and strategic thesis match.

Content Hashing & Caching

SHA-256 content fingerprinting ensures AI results are only regenerated when idea substance changes — intelligent cache invalidation.
